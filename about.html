ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Miheretu Teshale, although the picture is not mine, I'm pretty sure you will recognize me.

I am Miheretu Teshale. I am a student, and at current time I am studying software Enginnering at Addis Ababa University, at Addis Ababa institution of Technology.

I was born in 1991(EC) in city called woliso, which is located in the sout west of oromia region and it is called showa destinict. I begin school when I was 6 year old, and the first school is called Adeventist primary school and I began from KG 1, then a stayed in the same school until I transfered to Grade1.

I have lived around around three cities with my parents and sebilings in different times but most of the time we lived in Woliso.
